The .es3 file

It is better known as an e-dossier, but even e-authentic documents, electronic dossiers or "e-szigno files" are common.

An e-dossier works similarly to a compressed folder (zip or rar) that contains documents and electronic signatures.

The Hungarian electronic firm registry system is most often used, but it is also used in other official procedures.

It's free!

The es3.hu is a free e-dossier viewer. You can use it to extract and save the contents of an .es3 file.

Security

It is important to know that documents opened here will never leave your device, will not be uploaded to remote servers and the site will not store information about open files.
